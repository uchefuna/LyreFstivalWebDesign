Lyre Festival Food Menu

BARS

Lyre festival bring their critically acclaimed, family-owned food stall to VAULT Festival sharing their generations-old family recipes and flavours with you! Helios and Karla Gala serve up two dishes - first up is a healthy, hearty, and delicious bowl of rice, black beans, slow-cooked beef or chicken, fried plantains, avocado, pico de gallo and cornbread. And then there's the arepa; a traditional cornbread stuffed with your choice of meat or plant-based options and all the trimmings. If you're after something to share or a lighter snack, try the loaded nachos! All menu items are gluten-free and can easily be made vegetarian or vegan.

There's a plethora of immersive bars nestled across VAULT Festival with a unique range of drinks and vibes to give you the perfect experience for your night. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot for a pre or post show drink, a place to get on it with your mates, or a romantic place to bring your date, we've got something for you.
